Privacy Policy  •  Sleep Better. Live Better.

📋 Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how SleepWell ("we", "our", or "the app"), developed by Pavan Kumar Malladi, handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Last Updated: April 12, 2026
Effective Date: April 12, 2026

📱 Information We Collect

SleepWell collects only the information you provide directly:

  • Name — to personalize your experience
  • Age — to calculate personalized sleep recommendations
  • Gender — to provide tailored sleep health insights
  • Email address — to send you weekly sleep reports (optional)
  • Sleep logs — bed time and wake time you enter daily
  • Profile photo — optional, stored only on your device

💾 How We Store Your Data Local Only

All data is stored locally on your device only. We do not transmit, upload, or store any of your personal information on external servers.

Your sleep data, name, age, gender, and email are stored using Android's SharedPreferences — a secure local storage system on your phone. This data never leaves your device.

If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted from your device.

🔒 Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Upload your data to any cloud service
  • Use your data for any purpose other than app functionality

📧 Email Reports

When you use the "Email My Weekly Report" feature, the app opens your device's email app with a pre-filled message containing your sleep summary. This email is sent directly from your personal email account — we do not send emails on your behalf or have access to your email account.

🔔 Notifications

SleepWell may send local notifications to remind you to log your sleep. These notifications are:

  • Generated locally on your device
  • Not connected to any external server
  • Fully controllable — you can disable them anytime in app settings

🎵 Ambient Music

The ambient sleep music in SleepWell is bundled within the app itself. No external audio streams are accessed, and no usage data is collected regarding your music preferences beyond local storage of your selected track.

👶 Children's Privacy

SleepWell is designed for users of all ages including children. The app does not collect any data that is transmitted off-device, making it safe for all age groups. We comply with applicable children's privacy laws.

🛡️ Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Access — all your data is visible within the app
  • Delete — uninstall the app to delete all data permanently
  • Modify — update your profile information anytime in the app

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
