✨ App Features

😴

Sleep Tracking

Log bed time and wake time daily. Get personalized recommendations by age.

📊

Weekly Reports

Bar charts, sleep risk analysis, and personalized tips every week.

📅

Sleep Calendar

Monthly view with color-coded sleep quality for every day.

🎵

Ambient Music

Deep Sleep, Rain Sounds, and Ocean Waves to help you fall asleep.

🌙

3 Themes

Morning, Midday, and Night themes. Auto-switches based on time of day.

🔔

Reminders

Daily 8AM sleep log reminder and Monday weekly report notification.
